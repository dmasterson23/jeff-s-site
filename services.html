Services We Offer

Weddings

Master Sounds Entertainment specializes in creating a family friendly atmosphere at your wedding, while still making sure the party is hopping and the guests enjoy themselves! We meet with you to hear about your love story, design a plan and timeline specific to your needs and create multiple playlists of music that YOU want to hear! We know what it feels like to have an inadequate wedding DJ (ask us about OUR wedding story), and we want to make sure YOU have the wedding YOU want! We strive to make you feel comfortable.

School Events

School events are our JAM! We love to work with our local schools and deliver quality entertainment (100% clean music) at an affordable price! Working in the school systems ourselves, we know the demands and pressures that come along with your job! Worrying about finding and hiring a quality DJ doesn't need to be added worry- working with Master Sounds we guarantee students will have an unforgettable time!!

Private Parties

Looking to take your private event to the next level? Want to add something that all your guests can enjoy? Look no further then Master Sounds Entertainment. We will handle it all from music to games and activities! Your guests will love how interactive we are and you will love the stress free services we offer! We would love to be a part of your birthday party, holiday party, graduation bash and everything in between!
